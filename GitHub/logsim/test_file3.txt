DEVICES:
D1 is DTYPE;
D2 is DTYPE;
D3 is DTYPE;
C1 is CLOCK with 1 cycle period;
G1 is AND with 2 inputs;
SW1 is SWITCH with state 1;
END DEVICES

CONNECTIONS:
C1 TO D1.CLK;
C1 TO D2.CLK;
C1 TO D3.CLK;
G1 TO D3.DATA;
D1.Q TO D2.DATA;
D1.Q TO G1.I1;
D2.Q TO G1.I2;
SW1 TO D1.DATA;
END CONNECTIONS;

MONITOR D1.Q and D2.Q and D3.Q;
